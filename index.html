Objective of the webinar

Moving from theoretical hype to actionable execution.

Here are the 4 primary objectives of the APAC Data Centre Leadership Summit 2026.

01

Move from Hype to Actionable Execution

Shift the regional infrastructure conversation away from broad industry hype toward real, board-ready strategies that senior executives can act on immediately.

02

Drive Regional Thought Leadership

Establish NTT GDC as a trusted partner across South Asia, ASEAN, and East Asia by demonstrating how massive global scale combines with deep local operational expertise.

03

Address Core Enterprise Friction Points

Provide clear solutions for the "three-way pull" enterprises face today: scaling high-density AI workloads, ensuring data sovereignty & compliance, and meeting strict ESG sustainability targets.

04

Build a High-Intent Executive Network

Unite 650–800 verified decision-makers to set the benchmark for Asia-Pacific digital infrastructure in 2026 and beyond.
