Why attend

Three forces are rewriting APAC's infrastructure economics.

This is the room where senior leaders decide what to do about them — one focused hour, no slideware, every voice independent or enterprise-side.

AI infrastructure

AI is now a boardroom decision

Capacity, cost and capability have moved to the top of the house. The summit frames the choices CEOs and CIOs are weighing right now — not the procurement detail.

Data sovereignty

Sovereignty is reshaping roadmaps

Data-residency and control requirements are accelerating across the region. Understand what compliant, resilient infrastructure looks like in practice — and where the lines are moving.

Energy & sustainability

Energy defines the economics

Power, efficiency and ESG now drive data-centre strategy. See how leaders are balancing growth against sustainability — and what that means for the next investment cycle.
